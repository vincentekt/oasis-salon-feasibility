Oasis Hair Salon

Kaohsiung Market Entry & Feasibility Study

Target: Zuoying (Arena) / Lingya Districts Model: Premium Boutique Salon (MVP) Currency: USD ($)

1. Executive Summary

Recommendation Proceed (Boutique MVP)
Setup Size 800 sq ft
Initial CAPEX USD 90k - 100k
Monthly OPEX USD 10,000
Breakeven Volume ~4.6 Customers / Day
Average Ticket USD 80

Main Success Condition: Establishing the boutique salon in a prime commercial area within Zuoying (near Hanshin Arena) or Lingya (near Cultural Center/Sanduo) district. Easy parking access, modern storefront visibility, and high density of local affluent professionals are essential success factors.

2. Business Positioning

Positioned as: A premium, semi-private boutique hair salon specializing in creative coloring (balayage, highlight artistry), precision haircut designs, and integrated Japanese head-spa scalp treatments. Emphasizes artistic excellence and high-end hair health care.

Avoid being positioned as: A generic noisy neighborhood salon, a cheap local quick-cut shop, or a medical hair restoration clinic.

"Bespoke Hair Artistry. Experience premium styling in Kaohsiung's prime districts."

Core Customer Promise: Custom creative styling combined with medical-grade scalp detoxification using premium organic brands and advanced Japanese carbonated hair rinse systems.

Market Fit: Modern Kaohsiung residents seek sophisticated personal style and scalp care to counter urban pollution. We occupy a unique high-end niche that bridges artistic color fashion and scalp wellness in a relaxing, semi-private setting.

3. Market Context

  • Demographics: Over 2.7M residents in Kaohsiung, with high concentrations of high-income households in Gushan, Zuoying, and Lingya.
  • Spending Power: Tech professionals, corporate executives, and wealthy local business owners with high disposable income for premium personal care.
  • Environmental Drivers: Year-round warm climate combined with vehicle exhaust and industrial particulate dust creates high demand for scalp purification.
  • Competitive Environment: Saturated with traditional open-plan salons, but lacks boutique sanctuaries that offer semi-private styling and advanced hair treatments.
  • Licensing: Fits under standard hair salon business registration, avoiding complex medical or massage regulatory approvals.
