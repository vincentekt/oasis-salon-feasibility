Oasis Hair Salon

Melbourne Market Entry & Feasibility Study

Target: South Yarra / Fitzroy Model: Aesthetic Lane Spa (MVP) Currency: USD ($) / AUD ($)

Recommendation Proceed (Premium PoC)

Main Success Condition: Establishing our premium boutique head spa in a highly visible lifestyle hub in Melbourne (targeting South Yarra / Fitzroy) to capture affluent urban professionals, high-income retirees, and style-conscious residents. Sourcing Japanese-spec OEM equipment and focusing on premium organic scalp care mitigates high Australian setup costs, closing the local market quality gap.

2. Business Positioning

Positioned as: A premium, relaxing scalp wellness sanctuary combining high-end Japanese carbonated water therapy, soundproofed private relaxation suites, and digital micro-camera scalp diagnostics. Positioned as an elite, results-oriented self-care ritual.

Avoid being positioned as: A generic local hairdresser, a cheap express hair wash shop, or a clinical medical trichology office.

"Premium Scalp Therapy. Indulgent relaxation meets scientific hair health."

Core Customer Promise: Visible, verified improvement in scalp health and absolute physical relaxation under the guidance of fully trained scalp stylists using certified organic formulations.

Market Fit: Melbourne has a large demographic with high disposable income who spend heavily on wellness, skin clinics, and hair styling. However, there is a distinct gap for specialized, quiet scalp spas. Existing hair salons treat head massage as a brief add-on, while body spas lack the water-ring technology and diagnostics required for scalp detoxification. Our premium PoC closes this gap.

3. Market Context

  • Demographics: Concentrated wealth in premium lifestyle suburbs. High concentration of corporate professionals, wellness enthusiasts, and young families.
  • Spending Power: High local purchasing power. Residents are accustomed to paying AUD 150-250 for premium hair coloring and styling, making AUD 180 (USD 120) for head spas highly competitive.
  • Environmental Drivers: Exposure to UV rays, salt water (coastal lifestyles), and hard water in suburban supply networks leads to scalp irritation, dryness, and product buildup.
  • Regulatory Context: Compliance with Australian standards (AS/NZS 3500 plumbing standards), local council commercial zoning, and standard hair and beauty awards (Fair Work) is mandatory.
  • Market Gap: High demand for premium Japanese scalp rituals, but currently limited to very few, overbooked operators with multi-week waiting lists.

10. Customer Journey

  1. Booking: Seamless booking via Instagram / website, integrated with SMS notifications.
  2. Lobby Welcome: Welcome tea, brief intake form regarding scalp concerns.
  3. Microscopic Analysis: Scalp check using high-definition lens displayed on screen.
  4. Carbonated Treatment: Transition to private starlight suite. Organic botanicals, steam dome, carbonated water-ring cleanse.
  5. Blowdry & Styling: Professional dry-off, final comparison scan, membership package presentation.

11. Setup & Layout

The 800 sq ft space is optimized for visual flow, sensory relaxation, and strict plumbing efficiency:

  • Reception & Botanical Lounge (150 sqft): Warm lighting, gold trim, product shelving.
  • 4 Private VIP Pods (400 sqft): Soundproofed walls, starlight projection ceilings, Japanese-spec styling chairs.
  • Styling & Blowdry Station (150 sqft): Styling chairs and professional vanity mirrors.
  • Utility & Pump Room (100 sqft): Carbonated water system generators, AS/NZS 3500 compliant plumbing manifolds, laundry.

12. Staffing Model

Staffing models reflect Australian award compliance (MA000005) and standard loaded employer oncosts:

  • 1 Salon Director: USD 4,800/month (covers operations, bookings, marketing).
  • 3 Trained Stylists: USD 3,900/month base loaded each (highly skilled massage & treatment SOPs).
  • 1 Part-Time Assistant/Receptionist: USD 1,700/month loaded (handles laundry, check-ins, phone support).
Incentive Model: 8% commission on membership package sales to motivate staff and secure high-retention bookings.
