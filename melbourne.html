Main Success Condition: Establishing our ground-floor premium boutique hair salon in a highly visible lifestyle hub in Melbourne (targeting South Yarra / Fitzroy) to capture affluent urban professionals, style-conscious residents, and high-income shoppers. Sourcing premium styling equipment and offering customized master cuts, styling, and organic color services mitigates local market quality gaps.

2. Business Positioning

Positioned as: A luxury boutique hair salon offering customized master styling, premium hair cuts, and bespoke organic balayage/colour services in private or semi-private acoustic suites.

Avoid being positioned as: A loud, fast-fashion discount hairdressing chain or a clinical walk-in cut shop.

"Bespoke Hair Artistry. Elite styling and organic colour in an oasis of calm."

Core Customer Promise: Tailored styling and flawless colour transformations delivered by Master stylists in a relaxing, luxury environment using premium eco-certified hair care systems.

Market Fit: Melbourne's fashion-forward population has a high density of high-earning professionals and style-conscious individuals who regularly invest in premium hair services. There is a strong demand for luxury, quiet, individualized appointments that avoid the chaotic noise of traditional open-floor salons. Oasis Salon bridges this gap by providing an intimate, high-end styling experience.

3. Market Context

  • Demographics: Concentrated wealth in premium lifestyle suburbs. High concentration of corporate professionals, wellness enthusiasts, and young families.
  • Spending Power: High local purchasing power. Residents are accustomed to paying AUD 150-250 for standard cuts and styling, and AUD 300-500+ for premium coloring and balayage packages, making our average ticket of USD 250 (approx. AUD 385) highly competitive.
  • Environmental Drivers: While Melbourne's municipal water is soft, aging copper and iron piping in historic CBD, South Yarra, and Fitzroy buildings introduces heavy metal residues (copper, rust) and chlorine treatment byproducts. These metallic residues cause copper buildup in hair fibers, leading to brassy discoloration in blonde balayage and chemical damage, while chlorine strips natural oils and irritates the scalp. Local premium salons mitigate this with advanced multi-stage carbon and heavy-metal filtration.
  • Regulatory Context: Compliance with Australian standards (AS/NZS 3500 plumbing standards), local council commercial zoning, and standard hair and beauty awards (Fair Work) is mandatory.
  • Market Gap: High demand for bespoke coloring and balayage paired with holistic hair health, but traditional premium salons are loud, high-volume open-floor spaces with long waiting lists for top-tier master stylists.

10. Customer Journey

  1. Booking: Seamless booking via Instagram / website, integrated with SMS notifications and digital consultation preferences.
  2. Lobby Welcome: Welcome tea or premium beverage, brief consultation on hair goals and color history.
  3. Fiber & Scalp Analysis: Hair condition and scalp health diagnostics using high-definition microscopic lens to check for chemical damage and metallic build-up.
  4. Purified Backwash Ritual: Transition to our quiet backwash station. Multi-stage heavy-metal and chlorine filtration water rinse, scalp massage with organic botanicals, and cuticle-sealing nourishment.
  5. Master Styling & Finish: Precision cut, customized color/balayage completion, final style showcase, and package/membership presentation.

11. Setup & Layout

The 800 sq ft space is optimized for visual privacy and client flow:

  • Lobby & Retail Zone (150 sqft): Elegant reception desk, product shelves displaying premium organic haircare lines.
  • 4 Styling Station Bays (400 sqft): Semi-private bays, professional track lighting, styling vanity, 4 styling chairs.
  • Backwash Station (150 sqft): 2 luxury backstyling chairs with carbonated hair rinse systems and dim ambient lighting.
  • Color Lab & Utility (100 sqft): Color mixing inventory, water heater, carbonated system generators, laundry.

12. Staffing Model

Staffing models reflect Australian award compliance (MA000005) and standard loaded employer oncosts:

  • 1 Master Stylist / Salon Director: USD 5,500/month (covers operations, master cuts, high-end styling; approx. AUD 8,300/mo loaded).
  • 2 Senior Stylists: USD 4,500/month loaded each (handles cuts, colour, balayage; approx. AUD 6,800/mo loaded).
  • 1 Salon Assistant / Receptionist: USD 3,200/month loaded (handles laundry, check-ins, hair washing support; approx. AUD 4,800/mo loaded).
Incentive Model: 8% commission on membership package sales to motivate staff and secure high-retention bookings.
