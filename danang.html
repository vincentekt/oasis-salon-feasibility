Da Nang Market Entry & Feasibility Study

Target: Hai Chau / An Thuong Model: Coastal Expat / Tourist PoC (MVP) Currency: USD ($)

Main Success Condition: Establishing a premium boutique hair salon in Da Nang (Hai Chau district or near the An Thuong expat precinct) to capture retired Western expats, digital nomads, and affluent locals. Sourcing professional equipment and high-performing staff ensures a premium boutique experience with a lean, highly profitable structure.

2. Business Positioning

Positioned as: A premium boutique hair salon specializing in advanced color techniques (balayage, highlights), precision cuts, and luxury scalp-hair fusion therapy. Focuses on premium service quality and expert styling.

Avoid being positioned as: A standard generic beauty shop, a budget haircut shop, or a noisy low-end chain salon.

"Your signature look. Expert styling, soft-water hair care."

Core Customer Promise: Artistic hair transformation, high-end coloring, and deep scalp rejuvenation in a premium aesthetic environment.

Market Fit: Da Nang has a growing segment of Western expats, digital nomads, and affluent locals in Hai Chau who seek international-grade coloring (balayage/highlights) and styling, but currently rely on budget local salons or expensive resort day spas. Oasis Hair Salon bridges this gap by combining top-tier creative coloring with Japanese-inspired scalp hair therapy.

3. Market Context

  • Demographics: Da Nang has an estimated 15,000–25,000 foreign residents including South Korean business families (Hyundai, Samsung supply chain), European and US digital nomads, and hospitality sector expats. My Khe Beach and An Thuong area host the highest concentration of long-stay foreigners.
  • Spending Power: Korean business expats (highest segment) routinely spend $80–150/session on hair. Western nomads/creatives budget $60–120. Local Da Nang professionals' WTP is $30–70 but growing fast as the city develops.
  • Environmental Drivers: Da Nang's coastal UV index (9–11 in summer) and saltwater exposure from beach use accelerates color fading and causes hair protein damage. UV protection and color longevity are commercially compelling themes that no current competitor uses as a positioning strategy.
  • Competitive Environment: Premium salon quality in Da Nang City is sparse. Hai Stylist leads but operates in a functional rather than luxury format. Best quality is in Hoi An (30km), creating a clear geographic gap for a My Khe Beach boutique.
  • Regulatory Context: Standard Da Nang City People's Committee business registration + Ministry of Health salon hygiene permit. No additional barriers for chemical services.

  2. Welcome: Arrive in our cozy botanical lobby, served warm ginger tea.

  4. Treatment: Transition to private custom track lighting VIP pod for carbonated therapy.
  5. Blowdry & Close: Post-treatment comparison scan, blowout styling, package promotion.

11. Setup & Layout

The 800 sq ft space is optimized for visual flow, styling efficiency, and client comfort:

  • Lobby & Consultation (150 sqft): Coastal botanical design, retail product display, greeting area.
  • 4 Styling Stations (400 sqft): Premium styling chairs, large professional vanity mirrors, modern track lighting.
  • 2 Backwash Beds (150 sqft): High-end backwash rinse stations equipped with carbonated hair rinse systems.
  • Storage & Utility (100 sqft): Inventory stock room, laundry unit for towels, carbonated water system generator.

12. Staffing Model

Hiring qualified stylists in Da Nang is supported by highly affordable local salaries:

  • 1 Master Stylist (Director): USD 800/month (oversees salon operations, high-value styling consultations, and training).
  • 1 Senior Stylist: USD 550/month (focuses on advanced coloring, chemical treatments, and precision styling).
  • 2 Assistant Stylists: USD 350/month base salary each (provide backwash rinses, prep clients, and maintain cleanliness).
  • 1 Receptionist / Admin: USD 350/month (handles bookings, customer service, billing, and retail inventory).
Incentive Model: 10% commission sharing on membership package sales to motivate stylists.
