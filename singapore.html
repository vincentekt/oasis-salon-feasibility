Oasis Hair Salon

Singapore Market Entry & Feasibility Study

Target: Orchard / Tanjong Pagar Model: Premium Boutique Salon Currency: USD ($)

1. Executive Summary

Recommendation Proceed (Boutique Salon)
Setup Size 750 sq ft
Initial CAPEX USD 110k - 126k
Monthly OPEX USD 21,800
Breakeven Volume ~4.5 Customers / Day
Average Ticket USD 180

Main Success Condition: Positioning the salon in an upstairs or high-visibility ground-floor boutique space in Orchard or Tanjong Pagar to fit the USD 6,800/mo rent budget (approx. S$11–S$15 psf) while targeting high-net-worth professionals and expats. Ground-floor major mall units exceed S$30 psf and are cost-prohibitive. Flawless stylist skills and premium organic color products are critical.

2. Business Positioning

Positioned as: A premium boutique hair salon specializing in advanced color techniques (balayage, Highlights), precision cuts, and luxury scalp-hair therapy. It should operate in upscale, high-visibility upstairs or ground floor units to capture high-value creative color clients while optimizing lease viability. Focuses on premium service quality and expert styling.

Avoid being positioned as: A standard high-volume discount hair salon, or a purely therapeutic head spa. It must feel like a premium creative design studio.

"Your signature look. Expert styling, premium care."

Core Customer Promise: Artistic hair transformation, high-end coloring, and deep scalp rejuvenation in a premium aesthetic environment.

Market Fit: Surging demand for premium chemical work (balayage/highlights) and high-quality international styling among affluent professionals and expat communities. While Singapore has high-end hair salons, they often lack specialized scalp care integration and operate in crowded open layouts. We fill the gap by combining elite creative styling with luxury scalp care in semi-private comfort.

3. Market Context

  • Demographics: Over 1.5M high-net-worth individuals and multinational expats. High density of premium wellness consumers.
  • Spending Power: Elite global spending bracket. High willingness-to-pay for premium personal care, hygiene, and mental wellness.
  • Environmental Drivers: Constant year-round heat and humidity trigger scalp sweating, clogging, and sebum buildup.
  • Competitive Environment: Saturated with premium hair salons and luxury hotel spas, but lacks dedicated private acoustic head spa pods.
  • Licensing: Operates under standard salon guidelines, ensuring quick business setup.

10. Customer Journey

  1. Booking: Online via custom mobile app with instant schedule confirmation.
  2. Arrival: Welcomed in a luxury botanical lobby, served warm green tea.
  3. Scalp Analysis: Microscope scanner checks scalp health.
  4. Treatment: Transition to private starlight VIP pod, treated with carbonated water.
  5. Blowdry & Close: Post-treatment comparison scan, styling blowout, repeat booking prompt.

11. Setup & Layout

The 750 sq ft space is optimized for visual privacy and client flow:

  • Lobby & Retail Zone (150 sqft): Elegant reception desk, product shelves displaying premium organic haircare lines.
  • 4 Styling Station Bays (400 sqft): Semi-private bays, professional track lighting, styling vanity, 4 styling chairs.
  • Backwash Station (150 sqft): 2 luxury backwash beds with carbonated hair rinse systems and dim ambient lighting.
  • Color Lab & Utility (50 sqft): Color mixing inventory, water heater, carbonated system generators, laundry.

12. Staffing Model

Singapore's high labor costs require a highly efficient operations budget:

  • 1 Master Stylist / Director: USD 4,500/month (leads creative work, manages bookings, customer experience).
  • 2 Senior Stylists: USD 3,200/month base salary each (expert coloring, cut, and scalp-hair fusion therapy).
  • 1 Stylist Assistant: USD 1,900/month base salary (assists with shampoos, head massages, styling preparation, laundry).
Incentive Model: 10% commission sharing on membership package sales to motivate stylists and maximize retention.

To acquire clients in Singapore, we will prioritize localized channels:

  • Google Maps reviews: Proactive campaign targeting digital nomads looking for workspace relaxation.
  • Hotel cross-marketing: Place brochures in premium boutique hotels in Tanjong Pagar.
