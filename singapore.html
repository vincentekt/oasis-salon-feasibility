2. Business Positioning

Positioned as: A premium boutique hair salon specializing in advanced color techniques (balayage, Highlights), precision cuts, and luxury scalp-hair therapy. It should operate in upscale, high-visibility ground floor retail storefronts to capture high-value creative color clients while optimizing lease viability. Focuses on premium service quality and expert styling.

Avoid being positioned as: A standard high-volume discount hair salon, or a purely therapeutic hair salon. It must feel like a premium creative design studio.

"Your signature look. Expert styling, soft-water hair care."

Core Customer Promise: Artistic hair transformation, high-end coloring, and deep scalp rejuvenation in a premium aesthetic environment.

Market Fit: Surging demand for premium chemical work (balayage/highlights) and high-quality international styling among affluent professionals and expat communities. While Singapore has high-end hair salons, they often lack specialized scalp care integration and operate in crowded open layouts. We fill the gap by combining elite creative styling with luxury scalp care in semi-private comfort.

3. Market Context

  • Demographics: Singapore has 1.7 million non-residents and 550,000+ permanent residents, with the highest concentration of Western expats (UK, US, Australia, France) in the Tanglin, Holland Village, and Dempsey corridor. Japanese nationals (~40,000) cluster in Buona Vista and Holland area.
  • Spending Power: Singapore's expat community is among the highest paid in Asia. Senior professionals routinely spend SGD $300–600/session on premium color. The premium segment (SGD $200+) grew ~18% year-on-year in 2023–2024 per Singapore Retail Association data.
  • Water Quality: Singapore's NEWater system produces consistent, soft, low-TDS (TDS ~30 ppm) water — one of the cleanest tap water supplies in Asia. This reduces the urgency of soft-water filtration, shifting our USP to technique expertise, private bay comfort, and availability.
  • Competitive Environment: Chez Vous, Love Hair, Blonde Boudoir, and Shunji Matsuo dominate the premium segment. Combined they have fewer than 25 chairs total for the entire island's premium color market — chronically undersupplied against demand.
  • Regulatory Context: ACRA business registration + NEA food and salon hygiene compliance. Chemical services require Singapore's National Registry of Hairdressers qualification. No alcohol or food licence complications for a hair-only salon.

10. Customer Journey

  1. Booking: Online via custom mobile app with instant schedule confirmation.
  2. Arrival: Welcomed in a luxury botanical lobby, served warm green tea.
  3. Scalp Analysis: Microscope scanner checks scalp health.
  4. Treatment: Transition to a semi-private styling bay, styled with premium organic color and treated with carbonated soft-water.
  5. Blowdry & Close: Post-treatment comparison scan, styling blowout, repeat booking prompt.

11. Setup & Layout

The 750 sq ft space is optimized for visual privacy and client flow:

  • Lobby & Retail Zone (150 sqft): Elegant reception desk, product shelves displaying premium organic haircare lines.
  • 4 Styling Station Bays (400 sqft): Semi-private bays, professional track lighting, styling vanity, 4 styling chairs.
  • Backwash Station (150 sqft): 2 luxury backwash beds with carbonated hair rinse systems and dim ambient lighting.
  • Color Lab & Utility (50 sqft): Color mixing inventory, water heater, carbonated system generators, laundry.

12. Staffing Model

Singapore's high labor costs require a highly efficient operations budget:

  • 1 Master Stylist / Director: USD 4,500/month (leads creative work, manages bookings, customer experience).
  • 2 Senior Stylists: USD 3,200/month base salary each (expert coloring, cut, and scalp-hair fusion therapy).
  • 1 Stylist Assistant: USD 1,900/month base salary (assists with shampoos, head massages, styling preparation, laundry).
Incentive Model: 10% commission sharing on membership package sales to motivate stylists and maximize retention.
