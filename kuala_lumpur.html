Oasis Hair Salon

Kuala Lumpur Market Entry & Feasibility Study

Target: Mont Kiara / Bangsar Model: Premium Boutique / MVP Currency: USD ($)

1. Executive Summary

Recommendation Proceed (Premium Boutique)
Setup Size 800 sq ft
Initial CAPEX USD 90k - 101k
Monthly OPEX USD 9,145
Breakeven Volume 3.8 Customers / Day
Average Ticket USD 90

Main Success Condition: Establishing a premium boutique storefront in the Mont Kiara expat enclave or Bangsar high street, matching high-income corporate expats and local professionals who value artistic styling and advanced scalp-hair therapies. Convenient parking and valet drop-offs are key.

2. Business Positioning

Positioned as: A luxury boutique hair salon specializing in advanced color techniques (balayage, airtouch, highlights), precision cuts, and premium scalp-hair therapy. Focuses on artistic excellence, organic damage-free colorants, and personalized styling services in a relaxing, luxury environment.

Avoid being positioned as: A standard high-volume discount hair salon, a generic quick-cut salon, or a noisy low-end chain salon.

"Oasis Hair Salon. Your signature look, expert styling, premium care."

Core Customer Promise: Expert hair transformations and advanced scalp-hair wellness, providing an individualized look with zero hair compromise.

Market Fit: The high-income demographics in Bangsar, Mont Kiara, and KLCC maintain a high demand for premium aesthetic services, organic hair care, and international-standard coloring. By integrating upscale styling with advanced scalp-care rituals and damage-free color treatment, we capture a highly lucrative niche.

3. Market Context

  • Demographics: Significant expat populations from Japan, Korea, and Western countries concentrated in Mont Kiara, Bangsar, and Ampang.
  • Spending Power: High disposable incomes among multinational professionals and corporate directors. Allocation for routine self-care and aesthetics.
  • Environmental Drivers: High humidity and intense tropical weather cause sweating, oiliness, and dandruff, requiring professional deep cleaning.
  • Competitive Environment: Saturated hair salon chains (Number76, Miko), but lacking specialized acoustic head spa sanctuaries.
  • Licensing: Fits under standard hair salon permits, ensuring quick setup times.

10. Customer Journey

  1. Booking: Online via Instagram/Whatsapp with automated slot confirmation.
  2. Arrival: Welcomed in a cozy, wooden-accented botanical lobby, served warm herbal tea.
  3. Scalp Analysis: High-definition microscopic scanner checks sebum levels.
  4. Treatment: Transition to private starlight VIP pod, treated with carbonated water.
  5. Blowdry & Close: Post-treatment comparison scan, styling blowout, repeat booking prompt.

11. Setup & Layout

The 800 sq ft space is optimized for luxury comfort and operational flow:

  • Lobby & Consultation (200 sqft): Botanical reception area, luxury product retail shelves.
  • 4 Semi-Private Styling Stations (350 sqft): Custom styling stations with warm acoustic zoning and professional vanity lighting.
  • 2 Wash & Backwash Bays (150 sqft): Premium Japanese shampoo beds equipped with carbonated mist rinse systems.
  • Storage & Utility (100 sqft): Staff lounge, color mixing bar, water filtration, and laundry.

12. Staffing Model

Hiring qualified salon staff in KL is supported by competitive local base wages and performance incentives:

  • 1 Salon Director / Master Stylist: USD 2,000/month (handles senior styling, custom color work, and overall salon operations).
  • 2 Senior Stylists: USD 1,200/month base salary each (certified in advanced coloring and precision cutting).
  • 2 Stylist Assistants: USD 600/month base salary each (handles hair prep, backwash rituals, and salon maintenance).
Incentive Model: 10% commission sharing on membership package sales to motivate stylists and maximize retention.
