Oasis Hair Salon

Busan Market Entry & Feasibility Study

Target: Haeundae (Marine City / Centum City) Model: Premium Boutique Hair Salon (MVP) Currency: USD ($)

1. Executive Summary

Recommendation Proceed (Boutique Salon)

Monthly OPEX USD 13,400

Average Ticket USD 85

Main Success Condition: Establishing a premium boutique hair salon in Haeundae, Busan (specifically Marine City or near the Haeundae Beach commercial district) to capture wealthy local residents, premium domestic/international tourists, and affluent professionals. Establishing high-end styling stations and integrating scalp therapy services will drive robust local demand and guest retention.

2. Business Positioning

Positioned as: A luxury boutique hair salon in Haeundae, combining elite styling/color services (balayage, premium cuts) with an integrated Japanese-style head spa experience.

Avoid being positioned as: A low-cost franchise salon, a traditional beauty parlor, or a simple hair-washing shop.

"Luxury Styling & Wellness. Reclaim your confidence in Busan's premier coastal district."

Core Customer Promise: Bespoke premium styling and visible improvement in scalp hygiene using certified organic botanicals and premium Japanese equipment.

Market Fit: Haeundae (especially Marine City/Centum City) is Busan's most affluent enclave, home to luxury high-rises and high-income professionals. While there are local salon franchises, there is a distinct gap for a boutique salon that provides bespoke premium cuts and styling combined with a high-end, relaxing scalp spa experience using organic products.

3. Market Context

  • Demographics: Affluent residents concentrated in Haeundae (Marine City, Centum City) and premium domestic/international tourists.
  • Spending Power: High household income bracket with high willingness-to-pay for luxury styling, coloring, and scalp wellness.
  • Environmental Drivers: Coastal climate (humidity and sea salt) creates high demand for hair hydration, recovery, and scalp detox treatments.
  • Competitive Environment: Saturated with large domestic franchise chains, but lacking high-touch, intimate boutique salons offering private head spa integration.
  • Licensing: Standard Korean hair salon and aesthetic license required, ensuring straightforward business registration.

10. Customer Journey

  1. Booking: Online via Instagram/Whatsapp with automated slot booking.
  2. Welcome: Arrive in our cozy botanical lobby, served warm ginger tea.
  3. Analysis: Microscope check displays scalp health to the customer.
  4. Treatment: Transition to private starlight VIP pod for carbonated therapy.
  5. Blowdry & Close: Post-treatment comparison scan, blowout styling, package promotion.

11. Setup & Layout

The 800 sq ft space is optimized for visual privacy and client flow:

  • Lobby & Consultation (150 sqft): Botanical theme, organic product shelves, reception area.
  • 4 Premium Styling Stations (450 sqft): Acoustically isolated styling stations with professional vanity mirrors and Italian-style chairs.
  • 2 Backwash Beds (150 sqft): Soundproof partitions, Takara Belmont head spa wash beds with carbonated mist systems.
  • Storage & Utility (50 sqft): Carbonated water generators, laundry, organic stock, color mixing bar.

12. Staffing Model

Busan's premium salon labor market requires highly competitive stylist compensation:

  • 1 Master Stylist (Director): USD 3,200/month (handles premium cuts, client booking, staff training).
  • 1 Senior Stylist: USD 2,400/month (focuses on color, styling, and treatments).
  • 2 Assistant Stylists: USD 1,600/month each (handles shampoos, head massage, general prep; total USD 3,200).
Incentive Model: 10% commission sharing on membership package sales to motivate stylists and maximize retention.
