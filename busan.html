Oasis Hair Salon

Busan Market Entry & Feasibility Study

Target: Hai Chau / An Thuong Model: Coastal Expat / Tourist PoC (MVP) Currency: USD ($)

1. Executive Summary

Recommendation Proceed (Expat PoC)
Setup Size 800 sq ft
Initial CAPEX USD 85k - 100k
Monthly OPEX USD 11,000
Breakeven Volume 6 Customers / Day
Average Ticket USD 70

Main Success Condition: Establishing a coastal expat or city center storefront in Da Nang (Hai Chau district or near the An Thuong expat precinct) to capture retired Western expats, digital nomads, and premium coastal tourists. Sourcing high-quality Japanese-spec OEM equipment keeps initial setup costs extremely lean, matching local market requirements.

2. Business Positioning

Positioned as: A premium, relaxing scalp wellness spa combining Japanese carbonated mist systems, acoustic isolation pods, and digital scalp analytics. Positioned as a luxury wellness sanctuary for tourists and expats.

Avoid being positioned as: A local generic beauty shop, a budget massage center, or a noisy haircut salon.

"Coastal Rejuvenation. Calm your mind by the Da Nang shores."

Core Customer Promise: Visible improvement in scalp hygiene and deep physical relaxation using premium organic botanicals and Japanese technology.

Market Fit: Da Nang is Vietnam's primary coastal lifestyle hub, attracting a growing concentration of high-income Western retirees (MM2H/visas) and remote digital nomads. They prioritize wellness, relaxation, and self-care but currently lack dedicated, soundproofed head spa sanctuaries. Our MVP model is positioned to close this local gap.

3. Market Context

  • Demographics: Expats concentrated in An Thuong (Ngu Hanh Son) and affluent locals in Hai Chau city center.
  • Spending Power: Moderate local spending power, but high tourism and expat spending capacity.
  • Environmental Drivers: High sun, sea salt, and humidity lead to dry hair and scalp oiliness, necessitating deep cleansing.
  • Competitive Environment: Dozens of low-cost traditional shampoo parlors, but zero specialized acoustic Japanese head spas.
  • Licensing: Fits under standard hair salon permits, ensuring rapid legal establishment.

10. Customer Journey

  1. Booking: Online via Instagram/Whatsapp with automated slot booking.
  2. Welcome: Arrive in our cozy botanical lobby, served warm ginger tea.
  3. Analysis: Microscope check displays scalp health to the customer.
  4. Treatment: Transition to private starlight VIP pod for carbonated therapy.
  5. Blowdry & Close: Post-treatment comparison scan, blowout styling, package promotion.

11. Setup & Layout

The 800 sq ft space is optimized for visual flow and quiet ambiance:

  • Lobby & Consultation (150 sqft): Coastal botanical themes, retail display shelves.
  • 4 Private VIP Pods (400 sqft): Soundproofed walls, starlight projection ceilings, Japanese-spec styling chairs.
  • Blowdry Station (150 sqft): Styling chairs and professional vanity mirrors.
  • Storage & Utility (100 sqft): Carbonated water system generators, laundry, stock.

12. Staffing Model

Hiring qualified stylists in Da Nang is supported by highly affordable local salaries:

  • 1 Salon Director: USD 900/month (handles bookings, guest relations, accounting).
  • 3 Stylist Assistants: USD 600/month base salary (Omotenashi hospitality trained).
  • 1 Cleaning Assistant: USD 500/month (laundry, general cleaning).
Incentive Model: 10% commission sharing on membership package sales to motivate stylists.
