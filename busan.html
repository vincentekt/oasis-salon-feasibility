Main Success Condition: Establishing a premium ground-floor hair salon in Haeundae, Busan (specifically Marine City or near the Haeundae Beach commercial district) to capture wealthy local residents, premium domestic/international tourists, and affluent professionals. Establishing high-end styling stations and integrating scalp care services will drive robust local demand and guest retention.

2. Business Positioning

Positioned as: A high-end ground-floor premium hair salon in Haeundae, combining elite creative color (balayage, signature highlights), precision styling/cuts, and advanced scalp-hair rejuvenation therapies.

Avoid being positioned as: A mass-market franchise chain, a low-cost neighborhood salon, or a therapeutic scalp care clinic with no styling focus.

"Luxury Hair Artistry & Care. Reclaim your confidence in Busan's premier coastal district."

Core Customer Promise: Bespoke premium styling and visible restoration of hair and scalp health using professional organic formulas and advanced water softening systems.

Market Fit: Haeundae (especially Marine City/Centum City) is Busan's most affluent enclave, home to luxury high-rises and high-income professionals. While there are local salon franchises, there is a distinct gap for a street-level premium boutique salon that provides bespoke artistic styling and color services combined with specialized scalp-hair health integration in a high-visibility, luxurious environment.

3. Market Context

  • Demographics: Busan has ~35,000 registered foreign residents including Japanese (~5,000), Chinese, and a growing Western tech/education expat community at Busan National University and BEXCO district. Haeundae's Marine City luxury residential cluster hosts the highest concentration of high-income domestic and international residents. Summer tourist season (June–August) adds 3–4 million domestic Korean tourists to the beach area.
  • Spending Power: Haeundae HNWI residents (Marine City condos sell at ₩1.5–3 billion) have WTP of ₩200,000–500,000 for premium color services. Korean professional women in Seomyeon/Centum City budgets ₩150,000–250,000. Visiting Japanese tourists follow Happynian's ₩200,000+ pricing without resistance.
  • Environmental Drivers: Haeundae's coastal UV and seawater salt exposure accelerate color fading at 30–40% above inland rate. Hair protein damage from salt and chlorine exposure (pool use is high in luxury condo complexes) creates demand for bond-repair and deep conditioning treatments. No competitor addresses this as a core positioning strategy.
  • Competitive Environment: Happynian and Salon De Won are the expat-trusted choices but both located in Seomyeon, 20 minutes from the Haeundae luxury residential zone. ZS Hair is creative but not premium. Juno Hair is consistent but not boutique. The Haeundae Marine City boutique gap is the single most addressable market gap in Busan.
  • Regulatory Context: Korea Business Registration (사업자등록) + provincial health permit for cosmetic services. Foreign stylists require D-10 or E-7 visa with Korean Cosmetologist License equivalency recognition — work with a Korean licensing agency for foreign staff accreditation.

10. Customer Journey

  1. Booking: Online via Instagram/Whatsapp with automated slot booking.
  2. Welcome: Arrive in our cozy botanical lobby, served warm ginger tea.
  3. Analysis: Microscope check displays scalp health to the customer.
  4. Treatment: Transition to a semi-private styling bay with custom track lighting for carbonated rinsing.
  5. Blowdry & Close: Post-treatment comparison scan, blowout styling, package promotion.

11. Setup & Layout

The 800 sq ft space is optimized for visual privacy and client flow:

  • Lobby & Consultation (150 sqft): Botanical theme, organic product shelves, reception area.
  • 4 Premium Styling Stations (450 sqft): Acoustically isolated styling stations with professional vanity mirrors and Italian-style chairs.
  • 2 Backwash Beds (150 sqft): Soundproof partitions, Takara Belmont premium styling chairs and backwash systems.
  • Storage & Utility (50 sqft): Carbonated water generators, laundry, organic stock, color mixing bar.

12. Staffing Model

Busan's premium salon labor market requires highly competitive stylist compensation:

  • 1 Master Stylist (Director): USD 3,500/month (handles premium cuts, client booking, staff training).
  • 1 Senior Stylist: USD 2,500/month (focuses on color, styling, and treatments).
  • 2 Assistant Stylists: USD 1,700/month each (handles shampoos, head massage, general prep; total USD 3,400).
Incentive Model: 10% commission sharing on membership package sales to motivate stylists and maximize retention.
