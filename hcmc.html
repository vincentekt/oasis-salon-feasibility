Oasis Hair Salon

Ho Chi Minh City Market Entry & Feasibility Study

Target: District 2 (Thao Dien) / D1 Model: Expat PoC / MVP Currency: USD ($)

1. Executive Summary

Recommendation Proceed (Expat PoC)

Breakeven Volume 8-9 Customers / Day
Average Ticket USD 40

Main Success Condition: Establishing the PoC in Thao Dien (District 2) to capture Japanese/Korean/Western expat mothers and families close to their residences. The focus is to build a trust-based relationship with high-margin pre-paid packages rather than relying purely on walk-in tourist traffic.

2. Business Positioning

Positioned as: An expat-friendly, Japanese/Korean-inspired head spa in HCMC offering premium scalp care, steam treatment, oxygen mist, and sensory relaxation. It is a premium-feel wellness escape offering international service standards at accessible prices compared to Singapore or Tokyo.

Avoid being positioned as: A medical trichology clinic, a hair regrowth center, or a cheap local hair wash shop.

"Expat sanctuary. Rebalance your scalp, refresh your senses."

Core Customer Promise: Japanese/Korean-standard scalp care and massage techniques, verified with micro-camera scanning, in a quiet, hygienic, and relaxing English-speaking environment.

Market Fit: Ho Chi Minh City has a vibrant expat community with high disposable income but limited access to specialized, clean, English-friendly scalp wellness salons. Local hair wash places are very cheap but lack high-end technique and privacy, while premium salons focus primarily on hair color/cuts rather than scalp care.

3. Market Context

  • Demographics: Over 100,000 expats residing in HCMC, heavily concentrated in District 2 (Thao Dien) and District 7 (Phu My Hung). High concentration of Japanese/Korean expats.
  • Spending Power: Expats and high-income local Vietnamese earn USD 2,500+ monthly with significant allocation towards spa treatments, body wellness, and premium aesthetics.
  • Environmental Drivers: Severe air pollution, dust, and hot, humid tropical weather in HCMC trigger oily scalp, dandruff, and clogged follicles. Scalp detox is a health necessity here.
  • Water Quality: Municipal water is hard and often contains chemicals/heavy minerals that cause dry hair and scalp irritation. Hair spa detoxes resolve this.
  • Regulatory Context: Salons and head spas operate under standard salon licensing. Medical claims must be strictly avoided to bypass expensive and time-consuming medical clinic licenses.

10. Customer Journey & Sales Flow

  • Discovery: Expats find Oasis on Google Maps or via recommendations in local Facebook/WhatsApp groups.
  • Reception: Welcome with organic tea and warm towel in the lobby area.
  • Consultation: Scalp micro-scan shows oily build-up and clogged pores on tablet screen.
  • Service: Escorted to a private, soundproof pod. Flat-bed shampoo treatment with starlight ceiling, steam halo, and scalp massage.
  • Blow-dry: Dyson styling bar finishing. Second scan shows clear, healthy hair follicles.
  • Checkout: Stylist presents the pre-paid package offer based on the visible before/after scan results.
