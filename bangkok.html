2. Business Positioning

Positioned as: A ground-floor premium boutique hair salon specializing in advanced creative color techniques (balayage, highlights), precision styling, and luxury scalp-hair therapy. Focuses on premium service quality, expert international styling, and soft-water color protection to capture high-value creative color clients.

Avoid being positioned as: A standard high-volume discount hair salon, or a purely therapeutic hair salon. It must feel like an elite creative design studio.

"Creative styling, soft-water hair protection."

Core Customer Promise: Artistic hair transformation, high-end coloring, and purified hair recovery protected by pure, soft-filtered water in a premium environment.

Market Fit: Surging demand for premium chemical work (balayage/highlights) and high-quality international styling among affluent professionals and expat communities. While Bangkok has high-end hair salons, they use hard municipal tap water, which strips hair dye quickly and leaves mineral buildup. We fill this gap by combining elite creative styling with luxury scalp care and soft-water filtration to ensure color longevity and hair health.

3. Market Context

  • Demographics: 150,000+ registered expats in Bangkok; the Thonglor–Ekkamai corridor is home to Japan's largest expat community in Southeast Asia (~70,000+ Japanese nationals), plus a growing Western professional cluster in serviced apartments along Sukhumvit BTS corridor.
  • Spending Power: Japanese corporate expat families routinely spend ฿5,000–12,000/session on hair services. Affluent Thai clients in Thonglor match these rates. Per capita disposable income in this cluster is among the highest in Southeast Asia.
  • Water Quality: Bangkok Metropolitan Waterworks Authority municipal water has TDS of 300–500 ppm with high calcium/magnesium hardness. This is the single largest cause of premature color fading cited by expat clients in the area — yet not one competitor runs soft-water filtration as a core offering.
  • Competitive Environment: London Hair Salon, CYAN, Micha & Justin, and Yumoto Hair Salon dominate the Thonglor premium segment. All use open layouts; none offer soft-water filtration; wait times for complex color average 10–18 days.
  • Licensing: Standard Thai DBD business registration + provincial health dept salon permit. Chemical service workers require Thailand's hair dressing professional license (available via DTN exam). No significant barriers.
  • Foreign Shareholding Limits: Service salons are restricted to 49% foreign ownership under the Foreign Business Act. Setting up requires a Thai nominee partnership or applying for a highly restrictive Foreign Business License (FBL).
  • Nominee Legality & Disputes: Nominee structures to evade the FBA are illegal and subject to criminal liability. Shareholder disputes in Thai courts are highly unfavorable to foreign investors. Control must be secured through preference shares (10:1 voting rights) and debt control.

10. Customer Journey

  1. Booking: Online via Instagram/Whatsapp with automated slot booking.
  2. Welcome: Arrive in our cozy botanical lobby, served warm ginger tea.
  3. Analysis: Hair type and damage consultation to determine coloring & treatment plans.
  4. Treatment: Transition to semi-private styling station for custom coloring and soft-water washing.
  5. Blowdry & Close: Post-treatment comparison scan, blowout styling, package promotion.

11. Setup & Layout

The 800 sq ft space is optimized for visual privacy and client flow:

  • Lobby & Retail Zone (150 sqft): Elegant reception desk, product shelves displaying premium organic haircare lines.
  • 4 Styling Station Bays (400 sqft): Semi-private bays, professional track lighting, styling vanity, 4 styling chairs.
  • Backwash Station (150 sqft): 2 luxury backstyling chairs with purified soft-water wash systems and dim ambient lighting.
  • Color Lab & Utility (100 sqft): Color mixing inventory, water heater, multi-stage water filtration and softening tanks, laundry.

12. Staffing Model

Bangkok's moderate labor costs support an efficient operations budget:

  • 1 Master Stylist / Director: USD 2,500/month (leads creative work, manages bookings, customer experience).
  • 2 Senior Stylists: USD 1,500/month base salary each (expert coloring, cut, and luxury soft-water hair treatment).
  • 1 Stylist Assistant: USD 800/month base salary (assists with shampoos, head massages, styling preparation, laundry).
Incentive Model: 10% commission sharing on membership package sales to motivate stylists and maximize retention.
