Oasis Hair Salon

Hai Phong Market Entry & Feasibility Study

Target: Le Hong Phong / Vinhomes Model: Industrial Expat PoC (MVP) Currency: USD ($)

1. Executive Summary

Recommendation Proceed (Expat PoC)
Setup Size 800 sq ft
Initial CAPEX USD 30k - 38k
Monthly OPEX USD 6,500
Breakeven Volume 7 Customers / Day
Average Ticket USD 35

Main Success Condition: Establishing an industrial expat boutique in Hai Phong (near the Vinhomes Imperia or Le Hong Phong corridor) to target multinational corporate managers (primarily Japanese and Korean) working in the local industrial zones (VSIP, Trang Due). High-quality Japanese-spec OEM styling chairs preserve capital and respect MVP budgets.

2. Business Positioning

Positioned as: A premium, private scalp wellness spa utilizing Japanese carbonated mist systems, soundproofed styling stations, and micro-camera analytics. Focuses on premium service quality and deep sensory relaxation.

Avoid being positioned as: A local generic beauty shop, a budget massage center, or a noisy haircut salon.

"Industrial Hideaway. Reset your mind in Hai Phong's corporate hub."

Core Customer Promise: Visible improvement in scalp hygiene and deep physical relaxation using premium organic botanicals and Japanese technology.

Market Fit: Hai Phong is a major industrial port city with thousands of foreign industrial managers and engineers. They work long, high-stress hours and experience scalp issues from industrial dust. Currently, they lack high-end private head spas. Our model bridges this gap with competitive pricing and specialized services.

3. Market Context

  • Demographics: Significant concentration of Japanese, Korean, and Taiwanese managers in Vinhomes Imperia and Le Hong Phong area.
  • Spending Power: High corporate expat allowances and local middle-class expansion.
  • Environmental Drivers: Industrial dust, soot, and regional humidity cause heavy scalp sebum buildup.
  • Competitive Environment: Basic local beauty shops, but lacking premium specialized acoustic head spa sanctuaries.
  • Licensing: Fits under standard hair salon permits, ensuring quick establishment.

10. Customer Journey

  1. Booking: Online via Instagram/Whatsapp with automated slot booking.
  2. Welcome: Arrive in our cozy botanical lobby, served warm ginger tea.
  3. Analysis: Microscope check displays scalp health to the customer.
  4. Treatment: Transition to private starlight VIP pod for carbonated therapy.
  5. Blowdry & Close: Post-treatment comparison scan, blowout styling, package promotion.

11. Setup & Layout

The 800 sq ft space is optimized for visual privacy and client flow:

  • Lobby & Consultation (150 sqft): Botanical theme, organic product shelves.
  • 4 Private VIP Pods (400 sqft): Soundproofed walls, starlight projection ceilings, Japanese styling chairs.
  • Blowdry Station (150 sqft): Compact styling chairs and professional vanity mirrors.
  • Storage & Utility (100 sqft): Carbonated water system generators, laundry, organic stock.

12. Staffing Model

Low local salaries in Hai Phong support an efficient operations budget:

  • 1 Salon Director: USD 1,000/month (handles bookings, guest relations, accounting).
  • 3 Stylist Assistants: USD 650/month base salary (Omotenashi hospitality trained).
  • 1 Cleaning Assistant: USD 550/month (laundry, general cleaning).
Incentive Model: 10% commission sharing on membership package sales to motivate stylists.
