Main Success Condition: Establishing a premium boutique hair salon in Hai Phong (near the Vinhomes Imperia or Le Hong Phong corridor) to target multinational corporate managers (primarily Japanese and Korean) working in the local industrial zones (VSIP, Trang Due) and affluent local residents. High-quality premium equipment and integrated scalp therapies support the premium brand positioning.

2. Business Positioning

Positioned as: A premium boutique hair salon offering elite hair care, precision styling, custom balayage/coloring, and Japanese-spec scalp therapy integration. Focuses on high-end salon services, professional styling, and an exclusive lounge environment.

Avoid being positioned as: A basic neighborhood barber, a generic low-cost local hair shop, or a high-volume noisy salon chain.

"Precision & Elegance. Redefining luxury hair styling in Hai Phong's industrial gateway."

Core Customer Promise: Elite hair transformations, customized coloring, and personalized scalp health, using premium international products (e.g., L'Oréal, Davines) and expert stylists.

Market Fit: Hai Phong is home to thousands of high-earning foreign expat managers and affluent local families who demand high-end personal grooming and international styling standards. Currently, the city has many basic local shops but lacks a dedicated premium boutique salon offering consistent luxury standards and bilingual, internationally-trained staff. Oasis Salon fills this gap perfectly by offering high-end styling alongside specialized technical coloring and styling care.

3. Market Context

  • Demographics: Haiphong has ~20,000–25,000 Korean industrial expats (concentrated in Deep C, VSIP Hai Phong, and Nomura Thang Long Industrial Parks), 3,000–5,000 Japanese, and a growing European maritime/shipping management community. As Vietnam's second-largest port city, Haiphong's shipping and logistics sector attracts international professionals who are underserved by the local beauty industry.
  • Spending Power: Korean factory managers and Japanese executives earn international salaries — WTP of $50–120/session. Vietnamese maritime executives and young professionals in the port sector budget $30–70. This is a high-frequency repeat market if quality is delivered consistently.
  • Water Quality: Haiphong's HWACO water supply is drawn from the Cam River system, subject to seasonal salinity intrusion from Haiphong Bay and naturally high in dissolved minerals. TDS ranges 300–500 ppm with salinity spikes in dry season — particularly damaging for bleach-based color work. Soft-water filtration is both a real technical benefit and a compelling marketing differentiator here.
  • Competitive Environment: HaLa, Nguyen Hung, and L'Amour are the premium anchors — none at boutique luxury quality, none with Korean-language service, none with soft-water filtration. The Korean expat market represents entirely unaddressed demand in a captive geographic zone.
  • Regulatory Context: Standard Vietnamese LLC registration + Haiphong provincial health/hygiene permit. Korean/Japanese stylist work permits via standard 2-year renewable work permit process. No industrial zone restrictions on commercial salon operation in Minh Khai/Hong Bang district.

11. Setup & Layout

The 800 sq ft space is optimized for visual privacy and client flow:

  • 4 Premium Styling Stations (350 sqft): Equipped with high-end salon styling chairs, large LED back-lit vanity mirrors, and acoustic partition panels.
  • 2 Dedicated Backwash Beds (200 sqft): Premium backwash area with luxury recline basins, double-filtration soft-water rinse systems, and warm lighting.
  • Lobby & Consultation Area (150 sqft): Elegant botanical reception desk, high-end waiting lounge, retail display shelves.
  • Storage, Laundry & Utility Room (100 sqft): Water filtration system, laundry washer/dryer, product mixing station.

12. Staffing Model

Competitive local salaries in Hai Phong support an efficient operations budget:

  • 1 Master Stylist / Salon Director: USD 1,100 / month (leads the styling team, manages VIP clients, oversees salon operations).
  • 2 Senior Stylists: USD 800 / month each (base salary USD 800, specialized in coloring, balayage, and cuts).
  • 2 Assistant Stylists: USD 500 / month each (base salary USD 500, handles wash, scalp therapies, and client prep).
Incentive Model: 10% commission sharing on membership package sales to motivate stylists.
